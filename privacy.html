Privacy Policy — Clipbot

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Clipbot is a personal automation tool developed and used solely by the developer to edit and publish video content to their own social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube). This app is not distributed to or used by any other individuals.

Data Collected

The app accesses the following data through TikTok's and YouTube's official APIs, using OAuth 2.0 authorization granted directly by the account owner: basic account identifiers (e.g., open ID / channel ID) needed to authenticate API requests, and access/refresh tokens used solely to authorize video publishing on the account owner's behalf. The app does not collect, access, or store any data belonging to other TikTok or YouTube users.

How Data Is Used

Collected tokens and identifiers are used exclusively to publish video content, created and selected by the account owner, to the account owner's own accounts via the respective official APIs.

Data Storage & Security

All tokens and application data are stored locally on the account owner's personal computer. No data is transmitted to, stored on, or accessible by any third-party server.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party.

Data Retention & Deletion

Tokens are retained only as long as needed for the app to function, and can be deleted at any time. The account owner can revoke access at any time from their TikTok or YouTube account security settings.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children and is not used to process data belonging to minors.
